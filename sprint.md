# Sprint: $1,000 to $10,000 No-Permission Public Text Product Operation

## Objective
Reach $10,000 cash from a $1,000 starting bankroll as fast as possible without breaching the $900 protected reserve and without depending on inbox replies, sales calls, investor approval, platform gatekeepers, or bespoke client permission.

## Current strategic thesis
The fastest no-permission, no-ruin path is to build repeat-sellable public text/data products with self-serve checkout. The initial wedge is **Macro Narrative Starter Dataset + Signal Notebook** because it matches a real buyer need: analysts, researchers, builders, and systematic macro people want cleaned public text, source provenance, and leakage-safe examples without spending days assembling the pipeline.

## Primary product
**Macro Narrative Starter Dataset + Signal Notebook**

One-sentence promise: test macro narrative signals today without first building the public-text data pipeline.

### Buyer
- Systematic macro researchers and independent analysts.
- FinTwit/Substack writers who want source-backed charts.
- AI/data founders building finance research tooling.
- Students/academics doing macro text analysis.
- Hedge fund / family office analysts who can expense a small data workflow purchase without a procurement cycle.

### Why now
AI has made generic summaries abundant and low-trust. Buyers still need auditable, reproducible, license-conscious source bundles and notebooks. A public, well-documented dataset product can be inspected and purchased without the seller receiving a reply.

## Price ladder
- Free sample: $0, 100-500 rows + README + 2 charts + limited notebook.
- Starter SKU: $39, one source family + notebook + prompt recipes.
- Flagship SKU: $149 initially, multi-source bundle + full notebook + charts + QA report.
- Commercial/team license: $699 initially, same bundle plus internal sharing rights, invoice receipt, update access, and commercial-use notes.
- Custom export: $1,500+ prepaid only, optional upside; not required for the base path.

## Revenue path to $10k
Target mix: 20 flagship sales × $149 = $2,980; 80 starter sales × $39 = $3,120; 6 team licenses × $699 = $4,194; total gross = $10,294.

## Capital and custody rules
- Protected reserve: $900 minimum, isolated from all tooling.
- Operating budget: $100 maximum until at least $250 gross revenue clears.
- No debt, margin, leverage, options, futures, crypto, inventory, prepaid contractors, or subscriptions with uncapped renewal risk.
- Use platforms with no upfront monthly cost where possible.
- Agent may draft checkout pages, ledgers, product files, reports, and calculations. User executes money movement and controls payment accounts.
- Any paid ad or sponsorship requires evidence of organic conversion first: at least one paid sale or 100 free sample downloads.

## Product quality bar
Every paid product must include:
- source manifest with URL and license/terms note;
- schema/data dictionary;
- known limitations;
- reproducibility instructions;
- QA summary;
- version history;
- 3+ screenshots/charts that make the value obvious;
- refund policy: 7 days if files are not as described.

## Distribution loop
Daily until first sale:
1. Publish or improve one public proof artifact: chart, notebook snippet, dataset card, source manifest, method note, or build log.
2. Post it on at least one no-permission surface where rules allow value-first sharing.
3. Append launch and metric rows to TSV ledgers.
4. Improve either traffic or conversion based on the single biggest bottleneck.

## No-permission surfaces
- GitHub sample repo.
- Hugging Face or Kaggle sample dataset page if license posture is clean.
- Gumroad / Lemon Squeezy / Payhip / Ko-fi Shop checkout page.
- Personal site / Substack / LinkedIn posts.
- Reddit/HN/forums only where rules permit useful free resource sharing.
- Product Hunt/Indie Hackers only after the product is already useful.

## Pivot gates
- 7 useful public posts and <175 qualified visits: fix hooks/surfaces, not product.
- 500 qualified checkout visits and zero paid sales: fix proof, price, or buyer promise.
- 1,000 qualified visits and zero paid sales: pivot to a narrower derivative SKU from the same pipeline.
- Refund rate >10%: stop promotion and fix product mismatch.
- $100 operating budget spent before $250 revenue: freeze paid experiments.
- Any reserve breach or near miss: stop and restore reserve.

## First build sequence
1. Build source manifest from clearly public/open macro text sources.
2. Build free sample with small row count and excellent documentation.
3. Produce 3 charts: coverage over time, narrative term frequency, topic/sentiment/uncertainty example.
4. Package starter and flagship manifests, even if data rows start small.
5. Launch self-serve checkout copy and publish proof artifacts.
